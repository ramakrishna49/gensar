Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Gensar Consulting ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Fill out forms on our website (contact, career application, consultation)
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Submit a job application or upload your resume
  • Contact us via email or phone

The types of information may include your name, email address, phone number, resume, and any other details you choose to provide.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
  • Process job applications and recruitment
  • Send newsletters and updates (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law or as necessary to provide our services.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@gensar.com.
